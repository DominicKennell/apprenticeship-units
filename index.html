Stacked Units

Not just a course.
A development pathway.

We design stacked unit journeys — structured sequences where each unit builds on the last, creating continuous development, compounding skills and ongoing levy utilisation. When we talk about “stacking”, we mean internal development pathways we create with you, not a Government-approved credit framework.

  • Each unit builds directly on the previous one — employees develop coherent, deepening expertise rather than disconnected one-off training
  • Sector bodies expect that, in future, Government may allow units to contribute towards broader Level 4–7 learning, but no formal credit or stacking rules have been confirmed yet
  • Stacking keeps levy funds working continuously rather than in isolated bursts — maximising your investment throughout the year
  • Different teams get tailored pathways — a data team stacks differently to a marketing team or an engineering team
  • Employees can see a clear progression journey ahead of them — a powerful tool for motivation and retention
